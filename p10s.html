TLDR;

Dividing Wings

Bait the tether North on the marker, supports stack West, DPS stack East. Tethered Players will break their roles tethers.

1 Platform aka Bonds 1

Spread is like in the picture below, pairs is with your partner next to you and for roles you get together cardinally.

2 2 2 1 aka Bonds 2

Soak your tower, and then each laser will be soaked by 2 players besides the last one, order goes: 1: M+T 2: R+T, 3: H+T then T.

Note: There is a bad pattern, make sure to aim your laser diagonally as number 3 if the 4th turret is diagonal away.

Hector Guide

Raidplan

Pandaemoniac Bonds #1 #2 #3

Bonds 1: Soak your tower, and then each laser will be soaked by 2 players besides the last one, order goes: 1: M+T 2: R+T, 3: H+T then T.
Bonds 2: Same as Bonds 1 just in the middle platform.
Bonds 3: The boss will cleave either East or West dodge on the other side then Spread or Rolestack, the order is random (it cannot be pairs) then rolestack or spread again but this time watch out for the line aoes the laser shoot. You need at least 1 line between you and the other person if its spread.

Towers 2/2/2/1

Soak your tower, and then each laser will be soaked by 2 players besides the last one, order goes: 1: M+T 2: R+T, 3: H+T then T.
Note: There is a bad pattern, make sure to aim your laser diagonally as number 3 if the 4th turret is diagonal away.

Harrowing Hell

3 people will get the web that you use to get the tanks to the group. 1 person will get a stack and 2 people will get a tether, the same one as in the dividing wings mechanic. The goal is to make a line of webs in the back, twice, so it forms a wall making you unable to get knockbacked off the platform.
The 3 people with the web will go to the back, one on the east and west and one in the middle. The players on the edges of the arena need to make sure they touch the pole while the person in the middle connects the 2 webs. When resolved correctly, this will form the first part of the wall.
While that is happening, the players with the tethers will bait their cone AoE directly outside east/west, and the player who has the stack marker will stack between the 2 poles that give the tether. The 2 people who got nothing stack with the person forming a triangle towards the 3 people making a web.
Once everything resolves, the people who just stacked will get webs to form the 2nd wall. Go to your relative position where you stacked and form a 2nd wall, but make sure NOT to touch the first wall; otherwise, you will cut it.
Once the wall is made, tanks stand in front, and if healer lb is not used, you can tank lb3 here after the 3rd hit.
After the knockback, resolve your bonds in the same way you do bonds #1 and #2.
