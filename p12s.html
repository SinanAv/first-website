MECHANIC BREAKDOWN

Phase 1:

Paradeigma 1

From middle to wall:
MT > OT > H1 > H2
M1 > M2 > R1 > R2
Supports go to the side where adds spawn first and bait in that order, while that is happening the boss is cleaving half the arena 3x her wings indicate which side she is going cleave while turning around everytime.
DPS stand max melee south and then swap with the supports.

Paradeigma 2

4 players will get a tether and 4 players will get a debuff indicating the tower color they are placing.

Players with the tether just stretch their tether max melee of the bosses hitbox, while people with the debuffs need to make sure they place their debuff to the opposite color tether. Once the tethers and towers resolve, the tether people need to seak the tower that got placed next to them. After you soak/place the tower make sure you to dodge the Angels that got placed north at the start of the mechanic and dodge their line AOE.
