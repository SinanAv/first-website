MECHANIC BREAKDOWN

Hector Guide

Raidplan Note: The Light and Dark mechanic is different in the Toolbox for that please refer to the Video/Raidplan below.

Shadows Messengers

Group 2 goes East and Group 1 goes West.
2 clones will spawn on 2 intercardinals and will cast a light and dark divisive mechanic, the MT needs to make sure that the Boss faces towards the light clone. Then check whether the Boss is doing a light or dark attack.
Once the clones do their attack, dodge into the dark attack, then to the bosses mechanic. If its light you stay out and stack if its dark you dodge the charge and then dodge for pairs.

Light and Dark

Here is the Raidplan for light and dark, pages 10-13

Letter of the law

2 ads will spawn on intercardinals again but we will ignore them for now.
Tanks start North everyone else South, the Boss will cast Twofold revelation, orbs and portals will spawn, look for the dark savespot if its dark look for the light savespot, at the same time 2 players will be tethered 1 tank and 1 healer/DPS the tank that is not tethered will join the group in their savespot. The tethered tank has to go opposite of the group on the other side of the arena savespot.
The boss will cast Heart of Judgement and spawn towers but dont be too fast to rush into them, first the orbs will blow up and the portals will shoot after that the tank who has the dark tether will go to the middle, creating a savespot.
At the same time as the tank gets the tether buster the Party will get a stack and move immediatly to the middle and stand in the savespot.
After that everyone will go to their assigned tower, after soaking the tower the group will go to East and West dodge the clone aoes just like in Shadows Messengers, while you dodge press knockback immunity because the Boss is doing either light or dark with a knockback, if its light stand out and healer stacks, if its dark go in and pairs.
