Levinstrike/LC1 Raidplan

LEVINSTRIKE (LC1) (JP)

Raidplan Levinstrike/LC1 Raidplan
Tower order: 6-8-2-4
When orbs spawn with numbers, the party will position in between orbs #5 and #7.
Numbered players take note of the number on their head, taking towers in the order above and moving behind the party in order of explosion (2-4-6-8).
Non-numbered players will stay grouped with the party and look for the blue icon (defamation) above their head. When the first defamation appears, that player will run to the other side of the arena diagonal between the other two orbs (to the wall) and return to the party after the AOE resolves.
After the second defamation resolves, the party will swap sides and sit between the orbs on the opposite side (between #1 and #3) and the rest of the party will resolve their mechanics the same way, just mirrored.

Charybdis

Both DPS and supports take their thunder on their corresponding marker color, towards the tornado.
DPS Bait lightning first by going close to the boss and baiting it towards the tornados.
Supports bait the stack on the first clockwise broken meteor.
Supports swap with DPS and DPS go to the other meteor thats broken.

Chimeric Succession (LC2)

Players marked as 1 and 2 go NW and NE to the wall.
Players marked with 3 and 4 swap with players 1 and 2 respectively, 1 swaps with 3 and 2 with 4.
The other 4 players go to the south wall and stack up while the boss is casting frontstrike or rearstrike if the boss is casting frontstrike you have to bait him facing out, when rearstrike you have to bait him facing in after he jumps on you for the stack.
